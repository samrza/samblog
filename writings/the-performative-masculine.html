The Performative Masculine

what is Performative Masculine ? - a performative male is a guy who align is action and behaviours and lifestyle to rewarded by society or acceptance in society, totally to get attention and validation.

Performative masculinity is when men act in certain ways—like being tough, unemotional, or dominant—to fit society’s expectations of what it means to be a "real man." It's about showing masculinity rather than naturally being it.
