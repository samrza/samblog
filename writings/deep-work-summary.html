Deep Work by Cal Newport

deep work vs shallow work

deep work = rare , hard to replicate

shallow work = non demanding , easy replicable

that only need isolation and focus

rythmic approach work for me bcz I want deep work as a habit on the same time.

shallow work is easier - so mass prefer

busyness != productivity

a deep life is not just economically lucarative but also life well lived

1. deep work is valuable :

streamlines attention and increase happiness ( neurological )

generates flow that satisfies our life ( flow - psychological )

mindset of craftmanship ( builder )

those who will thrive are - high skilled worker, superstars, owners

four pillar of deep work :

1. two core abilities of thriving

a. ability to quickly master hard things

b. ability to produce at an elite level in terms of both quality and speed.

quality determines opportunity time and speed determines competition out.

how deep work enables mastery

1. deliberate practice - high quality work produced = time spent x intensity of focus

problem - attention residue

soln - work on single task for long period without switching

deep work is rare

a. the principle of least resistance - we naturally gravitate towards what easier at that moment.

b. busyness as proxy for productivity

c. culture of connectivity

deep work is meaningful

our brain construct our worldview based on what we pay attention to.

deep work focus your attention on thing that matter creating richer mental world.

RULE 1 : work deeply

willpower is limited, you have to establish routine.

strategies for work deeply - where / when / how

no distraction , metric for success, phone or other devices - nt

support : material organized in advance and ready

coffee or tea to initiate

4 discipline of execution (4dx)

1. focus on wildly important - one or two

2. act on the lead measure - for deep work track hours

3. keep a compelling scorecard

4. create a accountability

shutdown ritual - complete mental shutdown after particular time.

plan next day then completely off.

this is crucial for sustaining deep work.

RULE 2 - embrace boredom

the ability to concentrate intensely is a skill that must be trained

strategy 1 - schedule your internet use

resist and be bored at that interval

strategy 2 - roosevelt dashes

identify high-priority deep work

set a hard deadline and work until completion

strategy 3 - productive meditation

walking, jogging, showering, driving, dishes

obstacles : looping and distraction

soln : acknowledge and return

RULE 3 - quit social media

problem - any benefit mindset

soln - craftsmanship approach to tool selection

30 day social media ban challenge

don’t announce, don’t delete

ask : did it make life better ? did people care ?

RULE 4 - drain the shallow

don’t spend more time on shallow work

time scarcity forces better decision about what matters
